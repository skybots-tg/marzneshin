Frequently asked

Is DocuSwift really free?

Yes — single-file conversions up to 100 MB are free without an account.

What happens to my files?

Files live on our edge for the duration of the conversion and are purged immediately after the download link expires (typically 5 minutes).

Will scanned PDFs work?

Native (text-based) PDFs convert losslessly. For scanned documents we run OCR; results depend on scan quality.
